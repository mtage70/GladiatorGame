Privacy Policy

Effective Date:

This privacy policy applies to the Arena Manager app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created as a Free service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

Information Collection and Use

The Application does not explicitly request, collect, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) to external servers. All game progress, state, and preferences are stored locally on your device (e.g., via Local Storage or IndexedDB) to enable gameplay functionality.

We do not collect information such as your name, address, phone number, email address, or specific location data. Since the Application does not connect to backend servers to upload your personal data, your gameplay data remains on your device.

Third-Party Access

Only aggregated, anonymized data may optionally be transmitted to external services to help improve the Application. This Application may use third-party services that collect information used to identify you to improve the service.

Depending on the platform you downloaded the application from (such as Google Play), standard analytics and crash logs might be collected by the platform provider as part of their terms of service.

Data Retention Policy, Managing Your Information

Data is stored locally on your device. You can easily clear this data by uninstalling the Application or clearing the Application's storage data through your device's settings. Since we do not collect and store personal data on our servers, we do not have records to delete upon request.

Children

The Application is a game that may appeal to users of various ages. We do not use the Application to knowingly solicit data from or market to children under the age of 13. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that their child has provided us with information without their consent, they should contact us. We will delete such information from our files within a reasonable time, to the extent that any such information might inadvertently reach us.

Security

We are concerned about safeguarding the confidentiality of your information. However, since the Application does not transmit personal data, the primary security of your data relies on your device's security measures (e.g., lock screen, device encryption).

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time for any reason. We will notify you of any changes to our Privacy Policy by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to consult this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes, as continued use is deemed approval of all changes.
